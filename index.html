Hi, I’m a physicist who recently graduated from MIT. I’m currently researching ways to make a better X-ray laser with Dr. Marin Soljacic and probing difficulty of simulating 2D quantum circuits with Dr. Aram Harrow. In the past, I’ve worked, at PsiQuantum, on improving single-photon sources for photonic quantum computing and designed and built a way to probe scattered light on a photonic chip.

In my spare time, I design and build websites (like this one) and bike around Boston as long as the weather is nice.
